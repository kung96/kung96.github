ABOUT US


Travel International company Sdn. Bhd. established in 1995, we have been in operation for more than 22 years. We are a 100% Bumiputra company with all 3 licenses; Inbound, Outbound and Ticketing. We are a licensed Travel agency under Kementerian Pelancongan dan Kebudayaan Malaysia (KPL 2876). We are an accredited member of International Air Transport Association (IATA), MATTA and Malaysian Bumiputera Association of Tour and Travel Agents (BUMITRA).

Our core business is whole selling tour products mainly to Europe, Middle East, Asia, Australia, New Zealand and rest of the world. We are organizing schedule Series Departures to various destinations Europe, Spain, Morocco, Turkey, Egypt, India, China, Korea, Indonesia and other interesting destinations with attractive lowest affordable rate. We also provide Airline Ticketing services to almost all corners of the world on all major Airlines. We specializing in ticketing for group movements. Beside that we also handle technical movements (MICE) Government and Private companies.
